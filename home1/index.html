Top 10 Chess Players in History

Magnus Carlsen

World Champion and highest rated player in history.

Garry Kasparov

Dominated chess for over 20 years.

Bobby Fischer

American genius who revolutionized chess.

Anatoly Karpov

Known for positional mastery.

Viswanathan Anand

India’s first world champion.

Mikhail Tal

The magician of chess with aggressive style.

Vladimir Kramnik

Defeated Kasparov in 2000.

Jose Raul Capablanca

Known for natural talent and simplicity.

Emanuel Lasker

Longest reigning world champion.

Alexander Alekhine

Brilliant attacking player.
